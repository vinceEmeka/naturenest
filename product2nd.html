Ficus Iyrata commonly known as Fiddle-leave, fig banjo fig tree lyre Leafe fig tree, is a species of plant in the mulberry and fig family. The leaves are variables in shape, but often with a broad apex and narrow middles, resembling a lyre or Fiddle; they are up to 45cm (18 in) long and 30cm (12 in) broad (though usually smaller) with a leathery texture, prominent veins and a wavy margin
